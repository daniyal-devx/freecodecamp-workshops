Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Founder of Pakistan and leader of the Pakistan Movement

Here is Muhammad Ali Jinnah's timeline:

  • 1876 – Born in Karachi, British India
  • 1893 – Moves to London to study law
  • 1913 – Joins the All-India Muslim League
  • 1940 – Presents the Lahore Resolution calling for a separate Muslim state
  • 1947 – Pakistan is created, Jinnah becomes its first Governor-General
  • 1948 – Dies at the age of 71

"With faith, discipline and selfless devotion to duty, there is nothing worthwhile that you cannot achieve." — Muhammad Ali Jinnah
